Design Discussion

Modern Web Design

Our website follows a Modern Web Design language. The key principles of modern web design that we followed were to create a clean, user-friendly, and professional experience.

Contrast

Our website achieves contrast through the use of a light background paired with dark text and smooth-cornered cards. This ensures readability and draws attention to key elements, making the content stand out clearly.

Alignment

Alignment is consistently applied across our website. Headings are centered, while text is left-aligned, creating a clean and organized layout that guides the user's eye naturally through the content.

Repetition

Our design uses repetition to reinforce visual identity. Consistent card styles, button designs, and typography create a cohesive look and feel, making the website intuitive and easy to navigate.

Proximity

Proximity is used to group related content, such as the "Our Mission" and "Our Vision" sections. This helps users process information more efficiently by visually connecting related elements.

Hierarchy

Our website establishes hierarchy through varying font sizes and weights. Larger headings draw attention to key sections, while smaller text provides supporting details, guiding users through the content logically.

Whitespace

The use of whitespace between sections and cards prevents the page from feeling cluttered. This enhances readability and focus, ensuring users can easily digest the information presented.

Validation and Audit Results

The site passed the W3C validation test with no critical errors, ensuring clean, standards-compliant HTML. Accessibility testing using the WAVE tool confirmed proper use of semantic tags, heading structure, and contrast, resulting in a fully accessible experience for all users. Lighthouse audit results reported perfect scores of 21/21 in Accessibility and 5/5 in Best Practices, with SEO scoring 5/6. The single missed SEO item was due to the absence of a robots.txt file. Performance scored 2/3, mainly affected by uncompressed or oversized images. These results affirm the site’s strong focus on usability, modern coding standards, and accessible, human-centered design.
